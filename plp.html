What's Solar Energy?

The sun is the highest form of energy existing in our solar systems. Even though we can't capture the total of its energy, we can use a good part of it throught solar panels. F&C Energy offers a cheap alternative for the users to be able to consume energy produced by sun. This will both reduce your annual energy cost and also will contribute to decrease the global warming.
